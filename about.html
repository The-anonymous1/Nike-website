About us

Nike: A Legacy of Athletic Excellence



Nike, Inc., an American multinational leader in athletic footwear, apparel, and sports equipment, was founded on January 25, 1964, as Blue Ribbon Sports and later became Nike, Inc. in 1971. Pioneered by founders Bill Bowerman and Phil Knight, Nike made its mark with the innovative "Waffle" shoe design and rapidly expanded its retail presence since opening its first store in 1966. Known for significant advancements like the "Moon Shoe" and "Waffle Trainer," Nike also brought groundbreaking technologies such as Flywire and Nike Air to the sports world. Committed to sustainability, it has introduced eco-friendly initiatives like Reuse-A-Shoe and partnered with Newlight Technologies. As of 2023, with about 83,700 employees, Nike boasts a revenue of $51.22 billion and remains a dominant player in the global sports industry, dedicated to innovation, sustainability, and social responsibility.
